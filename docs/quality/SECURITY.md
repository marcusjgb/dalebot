# Security

## Mandatory Controls
- Authentication and authorization per resource.
- Multi-company isolation.
- CSRF, XSS, and injection.
- Rate limiting.
- Secure cookies.
- HTTPS.
- Secrets outside the repository.
- Signed webhooks and anti-replay protection.
- Logs without secrets or unnecessary sensitive data.
- Encrypted backups.
- Audited dependencies.
- Incident response.

High or critical risk blocks the release.
