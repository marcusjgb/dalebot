# AI Instructions

1. Respect PRD, rules, architecture, security, and ADR.
2. Do not invent requirements.
3. Do not add dependencies without justification.
4. Do not introduce microservices.
5. Do not place business logic in views or endpoints.
6. Do not disable security to resolve errors.
7. Maintain KISS, DRY with judgment, and YAGNI.
8. Modify only related files.
9. Add positive and negative tests.
10. Update affected documentation.
11. Report risks, assumptions, and decisions.
12. Do not hide errors or modify tests to make them pass.
13. Protect multi-company isolation in every query.
14. Prefer explicit and easy-to-maintain code.
