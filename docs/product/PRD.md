# PRD

## Problem
Businesses lose time responding to repetitive inquiries and coordinating appointments manually via WhatsApp.

## Value Proposition
Allow a business to configure services, professionals, and availability so their customers can book, cancel, or reschedule appointments via WhatsApp.

## MVP
- Business and user registration.
- Services and professionals.
- Schedules and exceptions.
- Daily and weekly schedule.
- Manual booking.
- Booking via WhatsApp.
- Confirmation, cancellation, and rescheduling.
- Reminders.
- Free plan and initial paid plan.
- Basic metrics.

## Out of MVP
- Generative AI.
- Drag-and-drop builder.
- Multiple branches.
- Mobile app.
- Bulk campaigns.
- Advanced CRM.
- Microservices.

## Metrics
- Average time to book.
- Conversation-to-appointment conversion.
- Confirmed appointments.
- Cancellations.
- Error rate.
- Active businesses.
- Free-to-paid conversion.
