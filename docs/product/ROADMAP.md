# Roadmap

## Phase 0 — Foundations
Documentation, local environment, CI, base security, and modular structure.

## Phase 1 — Identity and Multi-company
Users, businesses, roles, permissions, and data isolation.

## Phase 2 — Calendar
Services, professionals, schedules, exceptions, and manual bookings.

## Phase 3 — WhatsApp
Webhooks, interactive messages, state machine, and idempotency.

## Phase 4 — Operation
Cancellations, rescheduling, reminders, auditing, and observability.

## Phase 5 — SaaS
Plans, limits, billing, consumption metrics, and onboarding.

## Phase 6 — Pilot
Load testing, hardening, backups, restoration, and 5–10 pilot businesses.
