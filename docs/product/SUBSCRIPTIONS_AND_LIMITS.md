# Subscriptions and Limits

## Free
- 1 business
- 1 professional
- 3 services
- 30 monthly appointments
- No AI

## Starter
- Up to 3 professionals
- 300 monthly appointments
- Reminders
- Rescheduling and cancellation

## Pro
- Up to 10 professionals
- 1,500 monthly appointments
- Reports
- Configurable branding

## Business
- Higher limits and advanced features

Meta message charges, campaigns, and AI can be billed by consumption.
